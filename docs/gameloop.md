# Game loop

1. go train with your firefly
   
   1. gain money & stats, no matter the outcome

2. buy firefly upgrades in the store

3. breed fireflies

4. enter tournament
   
   1. on victory, gain a lot of money
   
   2. on loss, gain nothing

## Tournament

Enter a race battle, possibly with other players, and some AIs.

- Win by coming 1st place
  
  - Gain slightly less score by coming in 2nd or 3rd place

## Training

Enter the race battle map alone.

- Win by making a full lap
  
  - faster gives more points
